# Why It Exists

Context Tree exists because AI agents have no memory, and that is the bottleneck.

The models are capable. They can write code, review pull requests, debug production issues. But every time you start a session with an agent, it arrives with no context at all. It does not know what your team decided last week. It does not know who owns what. It does not know why things are the way they are.

This section explains the problem, the type of team that Context Tree enables, and the design principles behind it.
